## Overview

**FamSync AI** is a digital planning system designed to help modern families stay organized.  

Managing school schedules, appointments, chores, and shopping lists can quickly become overwhelming. This family AI Planner simplifies the process by providing a centralized platform for families to coordinate activities and responsibilities.

> “Technology should support families, not complicate their lives.”

<details>
<summary><b>🔍 Read more about the vision</b></summary>

The planner utilizes **neural scheduling**, **predictive reminders**, and **collaborative architecture** to reduce daily friction. Our mission is to help families focus on what matters most: **spending quality time together**. Over time, the system analyzes family behavioural patterns to provide increasingly personalized recommendations.

</details>

## Installation

1. **Clone & Install:** Download the application from the project repository.
2. **Create a family Account:** Establish your secure family administrative profile.
3. **Network:** Invite family members to join the shared planning environment.
4. **Link:** Enable calendar synchronization (Google/Apple) for full coverage.
5. **Calibrate:** Customize AI reminder preferences and notification thresholds.

After setup, the planner will begin organizing family schedules automatically.

---

## Usage

1. **Events:** Integrate school meetings or appointments into the master calendar.
2. **Tasks:** Create chore lists and assign them to specific family members.
3. **Alerts:** Activate priority reminders for time-sensitive activities.
4. **Monitor:** Track daily progress and responsibilities via the primary dashboard.

**System Note:** The core engine continuously learns family habits to provide **increasingly smarter scheduling suggestions**.
